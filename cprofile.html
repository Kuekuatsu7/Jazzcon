Manganese ore is a naturally occurring mineral, formed out of lava rock. It is extensively used in theproduction of ferrous manganese and ferro alloys. It is available in India too but we still import it due to the price factor, where the investment opportunity lies. The rock comes in lump form, with the major component being Mn or MnO, along with other minerals in small quantities. The price of the mineral depends upon the Manganese content in it, which may vary from 20-55%.All the logistics costs remaining same for all grades, the buyer prefers to buy higher grade than 40%. International pricing is determined in terms of $ / DMTU (dry metric ton unit), which refers to the manganese content in the particular ore. Even, for the purpose of calculating import duty, the government has fixed the duty calculation at $ 6.5/DMTU. With our efforts and connections, we have been able to establish supply sources, from where we can get the mineral in terms of $ per MT, instead of DMTU basis, which works out to be more expensive. On the other hand, we got potential and interested buyers willing to purchase if material offered to them at Indian ports. For transporting the material, we have lined up the logistics at appropriate rates. Now, we are looking for investment to execute this business for long term.
